What makes it tick

Tight controls, a calm-but-hostile reef, and zero noise.

One tap

Tap to puff and rise. Let go to shrink and sink. That's the entire control scheme — harder than it sounds.

Tight squeezes

Coral gaps don't care how puffed up you are. Time your deflation to slip through, then puff again to climb.

Beat your best

Every run logs your best score. No accounts, no leaderboards, no nag screens — just you versus the reef.

Quietly polished

Hand-animated underwater world with jellies, turtles, seahorses, crabs, and the odd scuba diver going about their day.
